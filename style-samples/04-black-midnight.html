Design Principles

Dark-first design with alternating slide backgrounds

Principle
Description
Dark-first
Black backgrounds dominate, with light slides for contrast and data density
Top-aligned
Content anchors to the top — no vertical centering for structured reading flow
Data layouts
Feature tables, property grids, and step lists — designed for dense information
Bold type
Archivo Black display font creates confident, high-impact headlines

The preset alternates between dark and light slides to create rhythm and visual contrast.

  • Full control over architecture

  • Faster deployment timeline
