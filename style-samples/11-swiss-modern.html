Design
Principles

Swiss design eliminates the unnecessary. What remains is precise, functional, and enduring.

Grid is law Every element aligns to the grid. No exceptions.
Form follows function Decoration exists only when it communicates meaning.
Typography as structure Weight and scale create hierarchy — not color or ornament.
Restraint is strength A single red accent says more than a gradient.
