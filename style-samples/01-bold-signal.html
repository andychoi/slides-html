Design Principles

  • Hierarchy first — Every slide needs one clear focal point that draws the eye instantly
  • Breathing room — White space isn't empty, it's the frame that gives content weight
  • Color with intent — One accent color used sparingly creates more impact than a rainbow
  • Typography as structure — Font weight and scale do the heavy lifting, not decorations
  • Motion with purpose — Animate to guide attention, not to entertain
