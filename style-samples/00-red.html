Design Principles

Top-aligned layout with structured reading flow

Principles

Content is top-aligned for a structured, document-like reading flow. No vertical centering — everything anchors to the top.

Top alignment Content starts at the top for natural reading flow.
Title barline Slim divider separates title from subtitle with equal margins.
Swiss foundation Built on Swiss Modern — grid, geometry, red accent.
Structured layout Predictable content placement across all slide types.
