Design Principles

Dark-first design with alternating slide backgrounds

  • Dark-first — Black backgrounds dominate, with light slides for contrast and data density
  • Top-aligned — Content anchors to the top for structured reading flow
  • Data layouts — Feature tables, property grids, and step lists for dense information
  • Bold type — Archivo Black display font creates confident, high-impact headlines

The preset alternates between dark and light slides to create rhythm and visual contrast.

Build vs. Buy

Evaluating the trade-offs between custom development and third-party solutions

Build In-House

  • Full control over architecture
  • Custom-tailored to workflows
  • Higher upfront investment
  • Longer time to market
  • Ongoing maintenance burden

Buy / License

  • Faster deployment timeline
  • Proven and battle-tested
  • Vendor dependency risk
  • Limited customization depth
  • Predictable subscription cost
